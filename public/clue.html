CLUE

Welcome to Clue!

This game is murder

It is a dark and stormy night at Tudor Mansion. While enjoying a nice game of hide and seek with some of your buddy, John Boddy's friends, the power goes out. When the lights come back on, everyone reconvenes, only John isn't around. Everyone moves to the hallway and....John has been murdered and his body's been moved!!!! You give the shifty eye to everyone there. Someone (maybe even you) is hiding something. Not wanting to be the next victim and not trusting one another, you all spread out in search of clues. Who did it? Where? And with what? Find out or you may be next!

        About

        I built this game using solely Bootstrap, JavaScript and jQuery. I decided to build Clue because I'm a bit nostalgic, but also because it required a lot of logic that I wanted to work through. It has been an awesome challenge and I hope you enjoy it!

        The patterns used on the background, paper, and leather are modified from Subtle Patterns. The notepad is based on a theme from http://Patrick Wied. The flashlight is based on a theme from http://megandalbey.com. Other than that, the rest is mine. If you like my game, feel free to view more of my work at http://forlorne.us
